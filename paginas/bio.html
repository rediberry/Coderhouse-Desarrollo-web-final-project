About me

Once upon a time in Villa Urquiza...

I was born in Buenos Aires Argentina in the mid 80s. My interest in music started since I was a child in the 90's with the popular music of that time. In the first years of the 2000's my interest in House Music started listening to numerous DJs in clubs and festivals with my friends.

At that moment I knew I wanted to learn how to make that kind of music. After a few years taking some production courses I started playing around making my own tracks, Remixes and DJ Sets. In the last decade started a deeper interest in African American music, the appreciation for Soul, R&B, Funk and Disco music was on the rise.

In 2020 was when I finally decided to send my productions to labels of the "Nu Disco" genre, they had an instant impact and I was able to sign with labels such as Young Society Recordings, Tropical Velvet, Disco Balls, Mango Sounds, Chopshop, Feverball, Spa un Disco, Etc.
